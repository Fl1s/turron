About

- A video recognition system that works like Shazam - but for video. It analyzes short snippets (2-5 seconds), breaks them into keyframes, and uses perceptual hashing to identify the exact or near-exact source, even if the clip has been edited or altered. This preserves the full context of the snippet and enables reliable tracking of original video content. NO AI!

File storage

- Videos uploaded by users are stored on the server for the purpose of search and analysis by the algorithm. Original files are never publicly distributed; only algorithmic results may be accessed by users;

User responsibility

- Users are fully responsible for the content they upload. Users agree not to upload materials protected by copyright without the rights holders permission;

Search results

- Results generated by the algorithm may be available for download. They are intended solely for analysis or preview of uploaded fragments and are not meant for mass distribution or as a source of full video files;

Removal upon rights holder request

- If a rights holder identifies uploaded material that infringes their copyright, they may submit a removal request to email "commercialfl1s@gmail.com". All infringing files will be removed promptly;

Free and opensource

- Turron is a free service with opensource code. The service is not monetized and does not contain advertising, reducing the risk of commercial exploitation of uploaded content;

Usage restrictions

- The service does not guarantee access to full original video files. Any use of search results for illegal distribution, commercial purposes, or copyright infringement is strictly prohibited;
